About This Project

This website lets you create a personalized amulet by blending AI-generated art with symbolic energy. Your birthday, your wishes, and your selected style combine to create a unique sigil.

The goal is not superstition, but personal intention, fun, and inspiration. These digital amulets are designed to reflect your current desires and aesthetic, using modern design and spiritual motifs.

Is this a real spiritual tool?

This is not religious or superstitious—it’s symbolic art powered by AI. Consider it a vibe enhancer or self-reflection token.

Why do you ask for my birthdate?

We use your birthdate to align your amulet with astrological and symbolic meaning. It’s part of the ritual experience.

Will you store my information?

No. Your input is processed in-session only. We do not store or track your data.
